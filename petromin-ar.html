Founded in 1968 as Petrolube, a joint venture between Saudi Aramco and Exxon Mobil, Petromin has grown from a lubricants manufacturer into one of Saudi Arabia's largest end-to-end mobility groups — now part of the Al-Dabbagh Group. It is headquartered in Jeddah and serves customers across the Middle East, Africa and Asia.
